fieldWorks sits at the intersection of digital methods and prehistoric landscape — mapping, terrain modelling, and the archaeology of the South Downs and beyond.

What you'll find here: LiDAR analyses, site essays, 3D visualisations, and occasional thoughts on what it means to look carefully at ground that has been meaningful to people for thousands of years.
